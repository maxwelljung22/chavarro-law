About

Danilo Chavarro, Esq., LL.M, M.PHIL.

Danilo Chavarro focuses his practice on Family-Based Immigration and Removal Defense and is licensed in the United States and Colombia.

He earned an LL.M in U.S. Law from Temple University Beasley School of Law in Philadelphia and, after admission to the Pennsylvania Bar, served as a law clerk in the Philadelphia Court of Common Pleas for the First Judicial District of Pennsylvania.

Before moving to the United States, he worked at a boutique law firm focused on tort law and taught as a law professor at Icesi University Law School.

He also holds a Master’s Degree in Philosophy and a Specialization Degree in Political Contemporary Thought.

Mr. Chavarro is a published author, musician, and multilingual professional who works in Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, and English. He lives in Philadelphia with his wife and two children.
